Bitmap/Raster
  - Pixels of different colour's
  - Resolution = # of pixels on a screen
  - Colour depth = number of bits per pixel
  - formats:
    - jpg, jpeg
    - png
    - gif
    - tiff
    - bmp
  - Good for real life photos!

Vector
  - Drawing with vectors
  - Mathmatical forumala - less detail - good for icons!
  - Never get pixelization :)
  - no colour gradiation
  - formats:
    - svg
